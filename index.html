To use this tracker with Google Sheets:

  1. Create a new Google Sheet with two sheets: "Regular Bosses" and "Fixed Bosses"
  2. Get your Spreadsheet ID from the URL (e.g., https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/SPREADSHEET_ID/edit)
  3. Enable Google Sheets API and get an API Key from Google Cloud Console
  4. Share your spreadsheet with "Anyone with the link" can view
  5. Fill in the settings above and click "Save Settings"
  6. Click "Test Connection" to verify your settings

Note: Your API Key is stored locally in your browser and never sent to any server.
